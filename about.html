How I Work With Teams

A few principles that show up in every project, regardless of size or scope.

  • Strategy must speak numbers. Traffic, SQLs, pipeline or retention — every idea connects to a metric we care about.
  • Deep work over busy work. Fewer projects, done properly, with clear before/after impact.
  • Systems, not one-offs. Every asset we create should unlock three more opportunities later.
  • Low ego, high context. I work best with teams who are honest about constraints and open about feedback.
